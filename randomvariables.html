8Normal/Gaussian(μ,σ²)Continuous\(f(x)=\frac{1}{\sqrt{2πσ^2}}e^{-\frac{(x-μ)^2}{2σ^2}}\)\(F(x)=Φ\left(\frac{x-μ}{σ}\right)\)\(μ\)\(σ^2\)\(e^{μt+\frac{σ^2t^2}{2}}\)
9Gamma(k,λ)Continuous\(f(x)=\frac{λ^kx^{k-1}e^{-λx}}{Γ(k)}\)\(F(x)=\frac{γ(k,λx)}{Γ(k)}\)\(k/λ\)\(k/λ^2\)\(\left(\frac{λ}{λ-t}\right)^k\)
