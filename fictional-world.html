The Shattered Crown: Revolution of Avalon — Timeline

Key events and their significance (with links to database entities)

Year Event Story & Significance (linked DB entities)
1770 The Hidden Bloodline King Aurelius Pendragon IX publicly reveals the royal family's Arthurian lineage. The monarchy claims divine authority through Excalibur, formally binding Avalon and France into one realm. Entities: Citizen, Faction, Artifact
1785 Rise of the Magical Estates Nobles organize into elite magical factions — Order of the Light, House of Pendragon, and Circle of Shadows. Intellectuals and peasants rally around Lucien Moreau (“Fire of the People”). This stratification seeds later unrest. Entities: Faction, Citizen
1788 The Famine of Frost A magical experiment backfires, triggering a winter that destroys harvests. Nobles hoard supplies in Resource Depots, prompting thefts and growing anger among commoners. The scarcity accelerates calls for reform. Entities: ResourceDepot, Decree, Estate
1789 The Spark at Montclair Riots erupt when Lucien Moreau and rebel-mage Elara D’Avenir storm Montclair and seize the Stone of Truth, exposing forged royal decrees. This act ignites the Revolution of Avalon. Entities: Decree, Artifact, Trial
1790 Formation of the Council of Magic & Justice The revolutionary leadership institutes the Council of Magic and Justice (led by Éloise Marten and Judge Renard Voss) to catalogue citizens, artifacts, and estates — the first organized attempt to track magical & civic records. Entities: Citizen, Faction, Decree, Artifact, Estate
1791 The First Magical Trials The newly formed Revolutionary Tribunal tries nobles and corrupt mages. Trials rely on evidence from the Stone of Truth; many verdicts become public spectacles. Records of verdicts and punishments become central to the archive. Entities: Trial, Citizen, Decree
1792 Decree of Purification The Order of the Light issues a decree aimed at purging “corrupt” magic. Spell backfires lead to mass casualties — the decree is later revoked and used as evidence in prosecutions. The incident deepens factional mistrust. Entities: Decree, Faction, Trial
1793 The Shattering of the Crown In a climactic confrontation King Aurelius unleashes the spectral host through Excalibur. Elara D’Avenir sacrifices herself to break the sword; the monarchy collapses as the Crown is shattered. This marks the turning point in both politics and magic. Entities: Artifact, Citizen, Faction
1794 The Pendulum War Royalist holdouts led by sorcerer Mordren Leclair attempt to use the Chronomancer’s Pendulum to rewrite key events. Time-warp tactics fracture loyalties and spawn new splinter groups, complicating archival integrity. Entities: Artifact, Faction, Trial
1795 Restoration of Balance The Council issues the Final Decree of Unity, forbidding further magical warfare. The Pendulum and other dangerous relics are sealed in the Grand Vault and guarded by the newly founded faction, the Guardians of Time. Entities: Decree, ResourceDepot, Faction, Artifact
1800 Age of Archives Reconstruction begins. Archivists and historians (your database builders) assemble the Revolutionary Database System to preserve records of citizens, trials, decrees, artifacts, and prophecies so history cannot be rewritten again. Entities: Citizen, Trial, Decree, Artifact, Prophecy
